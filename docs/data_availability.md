## Data Availability

The annotated dataset used for training and evaluation of the detection models
consists of **1,048 manually annotated gentoo penguins (Pygoscelis papua)** 
derived from UAV imagery.

The dataset is **not publicly released** due to institutional, logistical,
and environmental constraints associated with Antarctic field research.

Access to the annotated data may be granted **upon reasonable request to the
author** for non-commercial research purposes, subject to compliance with
applicable regulations and permitting conditions.

The software code and methodological pipeline are publicly available in
this repository under the Apache License 2.0.
