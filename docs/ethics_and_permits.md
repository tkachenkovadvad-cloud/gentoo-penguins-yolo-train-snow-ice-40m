## Ethics and Permits

All UAV-based data collection activities referenced in this project were
conducted in accordance with applicable international and national regulations
governing research activities in the Antarctic Treaty area.

Fieldwork was performed under an official permit issued by the
**Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine**:

- Permit series: **AP No. 093-23**
- Validity period: **20 February 2023 – 30 April 2028**
- Geographic scope: **Antarctic Treaty area (south of 60°S)**

The data were collected during **28 Ukrainian Antarctic Expeditions (UAE)**
within the institutional framework of the
**National Antarctic Scientific Center of Ukraine (NASC)**.

All survey activities were non-invasive and conducted in a manner intended
to minimize disturbance to wildlife and the surrounding environment.
